Export Spotify Playlists

Instructions

  • Get the playlist link from Spotify and enter it into the form above.
  • Select Text to export the playlist data to .txt format.
  • Select CSV to export the data to .csv format.

Public playlists can be exported automatically. To export a private playlist, sign into your Spotify account before exporting the playlist data. Make sure that the connected account is the owner of the private playlist.
